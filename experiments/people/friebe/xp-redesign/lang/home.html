xp::lang

PHP-based, compiled, feature-rich scripting language. Comes with namespace, annotations, generics, enumerations and optional type support. Extensible to support aspect oriented programming and design by contract principles.

Features

  • Namespaces
  • Optional types
  • Annotations (Metadata, Decorators)
  • Operator overloading
  • Generics
  • No more primitives
